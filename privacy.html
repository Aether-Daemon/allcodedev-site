Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026  ·  Applies to all AllCodeDev applications

Short version: AllCodeDev apps do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything runs on your device. No accounts. No analytics. No ads.

1. Information We Collect

AllCodeDev apps collect no personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information.

The apps store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Theme preference — whether you have selected light mode, dark mode, or system default. This is stored using Android's standard local storage (SharedPreferences) and never leaves your device.

No other data is stored, logged, or retained by the apps.

2. Internet Access

AllCodeDev apps are designed to work fully offline. All content — questions, reference material, formulas, and tables — is bundled directly in the app at install time.

The apps may request internet access solely to open external links you tap (such as the NEC viewer, PSI exam portal, or licensing authority websites). These links open in your device's browser; we do not collect or receive any information from those sessions.

3. Third-Party Services

AllCodeDev apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no SDKs or libraries that transmit data to external servers.

4. Children's Privacy

AllCodeDev apps are intended for adults preparing for professional trade licensing exams. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through our apps, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal information stored on our servers that could be breached. The only data associated with your use of our apps exists locally on your device and is subject to your device's own security protections.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our apps, please contact us:

  • Email: support@allcodedev.com
  • Website: allcodedev.com
